About Us


Commonwealth Moisture & Mold Specialists is a fully licensed and insured mold remediation and moisture control company serving the Chester, Virginia, area. We are a family-owned-and-operated, state-licensed contractor that employs knowledgeable inspectors who accurately measure the mold and moisture levels of your home.


Our Full Range of Services Include:
  • Mold Testing & Remediation
  • Crawlspace Drainage
  • Basement Waterproofing & Sub-Floor Systems
  • Structural Repairs
  • HVAC Duct Cleaning, Repair, & Replacement
  • Vapor Barriers
  • Insulation Removal & Installation
  • Termite Inspections & Treatments
  • Dehumidifiers
